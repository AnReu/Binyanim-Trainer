Small Hebrew Tools

Here you can find some small tools that help me learn Hebrew verbs:

Verb Analysis
A small test for guessing the tense and the binyan of a given verb.
Go-To Verbs
Tests if you know your go-to verb form for a given binyan, tense and person.
Verb Conjugation Practice
Conjugate a given verb in infinitive into a given tense and person.
